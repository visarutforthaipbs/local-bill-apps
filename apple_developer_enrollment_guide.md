# 🍎 Apple Developer Program Enrollment Guide

Enrolling in the Apple Developer Program for the first time can seem daunting, but Apple has simplified the process significantly. This guide walks you through every step to set up your account and pay the $99/year fee.

---

## 📋 Step 1: Decide on Account Type
Before you start, you must choose between an **Individual** or **Organization** account.

| Feature | 👤 Individual Account | 🏢 Organization Account |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Developer Name** | Shows your **legal name** (e.g., *Visarut Sankham*) on the App Store and code certificates. | Shows your **formal business name** (e.g., *BillNgai Co., Ltd.*). |
| **Requirements** | Basic identity verification (Passport/ID). | A **D-U-N-S Number** (a unique 9-digit business identifier, which takes days/weeks to get for free), and legal authority to sign contracts. |
| **Verification Time** | **Fast** (Usually approved in 24–48 hours). | **Slow** (Can take 1–3 weeks due to business background checks). |
| **Recommendation** | **Choose this** if you are a freelancer or want to get set up immediately. | Choose this only if you have a registered corporation and want the business name to appear. |

---

## 🎒 Step 2: Pre-Enrollment Checklist
Ensure you have these ready before you begin:
1.  **Apple ID**: An active Apple ID. 
    > [!IMPORTANT]
    > Your Apple ID must have **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** enabled.
2.  **Name Match**: Make sure your Apple ID name matches the legal spelling on your **passport or government-issued ID card**.
3.  **Payment Method**: A credit card (Visa, MasterCard, Amex) ready to pay the equivalent of **$99 USD/year** (plus local taxes, around ~3,600 THB depending on conversion rate).
4.  **Apple Device**: Apple highly recommends enrolling via the **Apple Developer App** on an iPhone or iPad, as it uses face/fingerprint recognition to verify your identity instantly.

---

## 📲 Step 3: Step-by-Step Enrollment (via iPhone or iPad)
This is the easiest and most modern method.

1.  **Download the App**:
    Open the App Store on your iPhone or iPad, search for **Apple Developer**, and download it.
2.  **Sign In**:
    Open the app, go to the **Account** tab, and sign in with your Apple ID.
3.  **Start Enrollment**:
    Tap **Enroll Now** at the bottom of the account screen.
4.  **Enter Personal Information**:
    *   Enter your legal first and last name (exactly as it appears on your passport/ID).
    *   Provide your phone number and address.
5.  **Verify Identity**:
    *   The app will prompt you to take a photo of your **Passport** or **Government ID Card**.
    *   It will also ask you to take a quick selfie to verify that you match your ID.
6.  **Select Account Type**:
    Choose **Individual / Sole Proprietor**.
7.  **Read & Accept Agreement**:
    Read the Apple Developer Program License Agreement and tap **Agree**.
8.  **Complete Payment**:
    *   The app will use **Apple Pay** or redirect you to complete the payment via App Store in-app purchase.
    *   Once authorized, you will receive a purchase confirmation email.

---

## 🌐 Step 3 (Alternative): Web Browser Enrollment (via Mac/PC)
If you don't have an iPhone/iPad or prefer the browser:

1.  Go to [developer.apple.com/programs/enroll](https://developer.apple.com/programs/enroll/).
2.  Click **Start Your Enrollment** at the bottom of the page.
3.  Sign in with your Apple ID (you will need to pass the Two-Factor Authentication prompt).
4.  Fill in your personal details (name, address, phone number).
5.  Select **Individual** as your entity type.
6.  Accept the developer agreement.
7.  Click **Purchase** to go to the Apple Online Store check-out page. Enter your credit card details and confirm.

---

## ⏳ Step 4: Verification & Approval Timeline
*   **Payment Processing**: It can take Apple up to **24 hours** to process the credit card transaction.
*   **ID Check**: If Apple's system flagged your ID check for manual review, a support representative may email you asking for a clear scan of your ID.
*   **Activation**: You will receive a welcoming email: *"Welcome to the Apple Developer Program"*.
*   Once you receive this, you are fully enrolled!

---

## 🏁 Step 5: What to Do Next (Once Approved)
Once approved, you can log into your account at [developer.apple.com/account](https://developer.apple.com/account/) to start the code signing process:
1.  **Generate Developer ID Application Certificate** (Step 2 in the notarization plan).
2.  **Export it as `.p12`** (Step 3 in the notarization plan).
3.  Generate your **API Keys** inside App Store Connect (Step 4 in the notarization plan).
