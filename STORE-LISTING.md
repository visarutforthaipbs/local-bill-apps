## Review Notes (สำหรับ App Reviewer)

```
BillNgai is a local-first invoicing app for Thai freelancers. No account or
sign-in is required to use the app.

TESTING TIPS
1. On first launch, you can press "ลองดูข้อมูลตัวอย่าง" (Try sample data) on the
   dashboard banner to populate the app with a full demo document trail
   (quotation → invoice → receipt), or complete the 3-step setup wizard.
2. The Pro In-App Purchase (non-consumable) unlocks Google Drive sync and
   extra tax tooling. The purchase UI is under Settings → พื้นที่ทำงาน
   (Workspace) → "ดูแพ็กเกจ Pro".
3. Google sign-in inside the Pro sync feature connects to the USER'S OWN
   Google Drive (drive.file scope) for syncing their data between their own
   machines. It is optional, not an app account, and not required to use
   the app or to test any other feature.
4. All user data is stored locally in the app container. The app makes no
   network requests except the optional Google Drive sync.
```
